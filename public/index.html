Ziph's

Law Visualizer


How to use:

1. Enter a webpage URL (preferably one with a lot of text). Make sure the link has 'http://' or 'https://'

2. Site will extract all the relevant text content from the given URL. This excludes links, titles and references.

3. A graph and the data of the most frequent 18 words used in the text will be displayed

Note: It may take more than 10s for server to process websites with a lot of text
